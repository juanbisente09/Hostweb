Beginner

Simply start your workout with some aerobic exercises like arm swings, leg kicks, and walking lunges. Alternatively, you can warm up by doing easy movements of the exercise you're planning to do. For example, walk before you run.

Intermediate

Intermediates are the next level up from beginners and fit into the category that the majority of the population falls into. If you've been working out consistently for the last six months or more,then you're most likely at an intermediate level.

Advanced

Advanced trainees use advanced exercises that challenge their bodies for increased strength, power, speed, and extra intensity to achieve added results.
